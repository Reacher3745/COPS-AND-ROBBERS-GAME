How to Play

The Damage Number Game

Based on the pursuit-evasion game of Cops & Robbers (Cox & Sanaei 2019). A vertex is damaged when the Robber leaves it — i.e. moving from u→v damages u. The damage number dmg(G) is the theoretical minimum the Robber can force.

Turn Order

  • Round 0: Cop picks any vertex. Then Robber picks any other vertex.
  • Each round: Cop moves first (adjacent or pass), then Robber moves (adjacent or pass).
  • Capture = Cop lands on Robber's vertex. Robber cannot land on Cop.

Goals

  • Cop: Minimize total damaged vertices. Capture ends the game.
  • Robber: Maximize distinct damaged vertices. Avoid capture as long as useful.

1 Player Mode

The AI uses minimax search — it evaluates all future game states and plays the move that leads to the optimal outcome. As Cop it minimizes damage; as Robber it maximizes damage.

Key Theorem (Cox & Sanaei 2019)

⌊rad(G)/2⌋ ≤ dmg(G) ≤ n − Δ − 1
